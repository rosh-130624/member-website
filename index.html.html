FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying. Still have questions? Contact us directly.

How much will I actually receive after deductions?
You will receive the loan amount minus: (1) Service fee of 2% of the loan amount, (2) Insurance of ₱1,235 per year, and (3) Share capital of 5% of the loan amount. Use our Loan Calculator above to see the exact amount you will receive before applying.
What is the difference between the two payment methods?
Diminishing Annuity means you pay the same amount every period — easy to budget. Diminishing Balance means your payment decreases every period as your balance goes down — you pay less total interest overall. You can choose whichever method suits you better. Use the calculator to compare them side by side.
Can I pay earlier than my scheduled term?
Yes! Early payment is allowed and even encouraged. Since we use diminishing interest, paying early means you pay less interest overall. There are no penalties for early payment. Just inform your assigned collector or visit our office.
What happens if I miss a payment?
A missed payment may incur a penalty charge. We encourage members to inform their collector in advance if they are having difficulty paying on time. We are willing to discuss restructuring options for members facing genuine financial hardship.
What happens to my loan if I pass away?
Your loan is fully covered by the cooperative's insurance program. If a borrower passes away or becomes permanently disabled, the remaining loan balance is automatically waived. Your family will not be required to pay the remaining balance. This is why the insurance fee is charged — it protects your loved ones.
Can I apply for another loan while paying my current one?
It depends on your current balance and payment history. Members with a good payment record may be eligible for a second loan or a loan top-up. Please visit our office or contact your assigned collector for evaluation. Approval is subject to the cooperative's lending policy.
Why do I need 2 co-makers?
Co-makers serve as guarantors for your loan. If you are unable to pay, the co-makers are responsible for the remaining balance. Having 2 co-makers gives the cooperative added assurance and helps you get approved faster. Co-makers must have valid IDs and ideally be members of the cooperative or residents of Southern Leyte.
How long does loan approval take?
Most loan applications are reviewed within 1–2 business days. You will receive an SMS notification once your application has been processed. For larger loan amounts or incomplete requirements, processing may take a few more days. Submitting complete requirements speeds up the process significantly.
